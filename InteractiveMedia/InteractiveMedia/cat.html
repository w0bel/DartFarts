Week One

These drawings are my initial eight ideas for website designs. I decided not to go with any of them however, as I felt I was more inspired by my housemates cat than any of these ideas which I drew up.

During the first week I thought back to Neal Stephenson's book Snow Crash (1992) and William Gibson's 1984 novel Neuromancer. I was reminded of these two books as we discussed the new Apple Vision Pros during the class. Both books although written at the end of the 20th centruy, somewhat predicted today's technology. In Snow Crash people are able to "goggle in" to the Metaverse, a virtual reality world. Similarly in Neuromancer, the main character Case is able to "jack in" to cyberspace and his companion Molly has surgically implatent mirrorshades which enhance her vision. It's interesting to see what what was fiction during the 80s-90s is becoming reality, especially when both books are so dystopian.
